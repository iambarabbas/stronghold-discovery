Risk Mitigation

Risk

Form Fix Takes Longer Than Expected

Mitigation: If root cause isn't found in Week 1, consider rebuilding form on dedicated page rather than debugging modal. Speed matters more than elegance.

Risk

Google Ad Grant Denied or Restricted

Mitigation: Ensure compliance before applying. Have backup plan for modest paid search budget ($1-2K/month) if needed.

Risk

Content Doesn't Rank

Mitigation: SEO is long-term. Pair with Google Ad Grant for immediate traffic while organic builds. Focus on low-competition long-tail keywords first.
