Strategic Implications

What This Data Tells Us

  1. Double down on podcasts: 46.3% acquisition from this channel. MrBallen specifically is driving donors. Pursue more podcast appearances.
  2. Stories convert: 84.9% want field stories, 48.7% were motivated by a specific rescue story. Lead with narrative content.
  3. Faith matters: 47.2% are faith-motivated. Continue faith-aligned messaging for this core audience.
  4. Trust is earned: On-ground presence (60.8%) and leadership credibility (57%) drive trust. Keep showing real operations.
  5. Create engagement pathways: Donors want to do more than give. Build volunteer/advocacy programs.
  6. Your base is loyal: NPS of 85 is exceptional. These supporters will refer others‚Äîmake it easy for them.
  7. The form issue is real: 97.3% of successful donors had a good experience, but the 98.6% who couldn't complete never made it this far.

Voice of Donor Summary

Your donors are passionate advocates (NPS 85), primarily discovered you through podcasts (46%), are motivated by rescue stories and faith (48%/47%), and trust you because of your on-ground presence and leadership credibility.
