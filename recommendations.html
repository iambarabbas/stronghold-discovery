DO NOT add CTAs or increase ad spend until the donation form is fixed. At 1.4% conversion, scaling traffic wastes budget and frustrates supporters who want to give but cannot. Fix the infrastructure first, then scale.

Fix the Donation Form

The 98.6% abandonment rate is catastrophic. This is not optimization—it's emergency repair.

Target: Achieve 20-30% conversion within first week of fix

Implement Failed Payment Recovery

6.94% payment failure rate = $258,012 in lost revenue. Industry standard is 3-5%.

Actions:

  • Implement card updater service (Visa Account Updater, Mastercard Automatic Billing Updater)
  • Create "payment failed" email sequence with easy card update link
  • Add ACH/bank transfer as backup payment method

Target: Reduce failure rate from 6.94% to 3.5%

$100,000+/year recovered Low effort Week 2-3
