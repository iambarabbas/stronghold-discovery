Recommendations

  • 1. Improve Click-to-Open Rate
    Current 6.9% CTOR is below the 10% nonprofit benchmark. Emails are being opened but content isn't compelling action. Solution: Shorter emails, clearer single CTA above the fold, more video content.
  • 2. Reduce Unsubscribe Rate
    0.6% per email (3x industry average) means losing ~60 subscribers monthly. Over a year, this is 7-8% list churn. Solution: Segment list by engagement, send less frequently to low-engagers, add preference center.
  • 3. Double Down on Video
    Video-focused emails achieve highest CTOR (8-9%). Solution: Lead with video thumbnail and "Watch" CTA in every email. Consider video-first strategy.
  • 4. Standardize "By the Numbers" Reports
    Monthly ambulance reports consistently perform well. Solution: Create template, branded design, reliable monthly cadence. This is your signature content.
  • 5. Remove Social Links from Email Body
    Social clicks average 1-29 per email ‚Äî not worth the visual clutter. Solution: Move social icons to footer only. Focus email real estate on primary CTA.
  • 6. Segment Year-End Appeals
    "Thank You for a Year of Impact" got 46% opens but only 3.2% CTOR. Solution: For gratitude emails, add clear donation CTA. Split test: pure gratitude vs gratitude + ask.
  • 7. Grow the List
    Net growth of only 2% YoY won't sustain the donor base. Solution: Add email capture to website (popup, exit intent), create lead magnet (Burma situation report), cross-promote on social.

Bottom Line

Stronghold's email program is healthy but has untapped potential.

The good: 99% deliverability, 39% open rates (exceptional), consistent content calendar, engaged core audience.

The opportunity: Converting opens to clicks. With 4,000 people opening each email but only 250-350 clicking, there's a 10x engagement opportunity sitting in the inbox.

Priority fix: Shorten emails, lead with video, single clear CTA. Test this on the next 3 campaigns and measure CTOR improvement.
