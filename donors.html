Recovery Strategy

  • Implement card updater service: Automatic updates for expired cards. ~$100K recoverable annually.

  • Multiple payment methods: ACH/bank transfer as backup option
  • Target: Reduce failure rate from 6.94% to 3.5% = $129K additional revenue per year
